The Sunrise Cafe for the Homeless

The Sunrise Cafe for the Homeless is a breakfast cafe located in Seattle where only the local homeless population staying at the Sunrise Homeless Shelter is employed as a barista. All earnings go back to the workers here to help those less fortunate back on their feet. The cafe itself stays open through financial donations from local philantropists.

Feel free to come in sometime and help us make the world a better place. It can be just for a quick bite to eat or to participate in one of our several weekly activities. We would love to have you
